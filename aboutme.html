A little about me...

My name is Florence Poh and I'm 23 years old. I'm from Malaysia and I've been living in Ireland for the past 5 years. I came to Dublin, Ireland when I was 17 years old to study a Foundation Programme in Business and then continued with a Bachelor Degree in Journalism and Visual Media in Griffith College.

I graduated in November 2017 and I am currently pursuing a Masters in Applied Digital Media. I am a very open-minded person and I like to experience and try new things.

I love to play the piano when I'm not coding.
